Welcome to SNG

SNG (System and Networking Group) is from School of Informatics at XMU (Xiamen University), led by Prof. Qiao Xiang.

The team at SNG focus on the following research areas:

  • Network Verification
  • Network Programming
  • Interpretable Machine Learning
  • Congestion Control

We have openings for Master students and PhD students with research interests in the areas above. You are welcome to contact Prof. Qiao Xiang.
